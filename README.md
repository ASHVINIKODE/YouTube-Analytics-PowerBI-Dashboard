# 📊 YouTube Analytics Power BI Dashboard

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project presents an **End-to-End YouTube Analytics Dashboard** developed using **Power BI** to analyze content performance, viewer engagement and creator growth.

The objective is to transform raw YouTube data into **actionable business insights** that help optimize content strategy, improve retention, and identify growth opportunities.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Analyze viewer watch-time and retention patterns
* Evaluate creator performance and channel growth
* Understand country-wise audience behavior
* Study the impact of video duration on engagement

---

## 📊 Dashboards Included

### 1️⃣ Watch-Time & Retention Dashboard

**Objective:** Analyze viewer behavior to improve content performance.

**Key KPIs**

* Total Watch Time
* Average View Duration
* Retention Rate
* Drop-off Percentage

**Visualizations**

* Line Chart – Watch Time vs Video Duration
* Funnel Chart – Viewer Drop-off Stages
* Bar Chart – Category-wise Retention
* KPI Cards

**Insights**

* Optimal video length for higher retention
* Categories with lowest viewer drop-off
* Engagement impact on watch time

---

### 2️⃣ Creator Performance Dashboard

**Objective:** Evaluate creator growth and influence.

**Key KPIs**

* Total Subscribers
* Subscriber Growth Rate
* Upload Frequency
* Views per Upload

**Visualizations**

* Radar Chart – Channel Type Performance
* Bar Chart – Top Creators by Subscribers
* Line Chart – Subscriber Growth Trend
* Ranking Table using DAX

**Insights**

* Most consistent creators
* High-performing channel categories
* Upload frequency vs channel growth
